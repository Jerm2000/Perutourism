This point of this website is to save vacationers going to Peru the annoyance of getting to their destination and having to waste a whole day wandering about with no goal in mind. Through the articles and pictures, I hope to provide you with the inspiration needed to "seize the day" and make the most of your time in this beautiful and history filled country.

Welcome! If you're reading this, you're most likely planning a trip to Peru. Having visited Peru on multiple occasions myself, I know that it can be frustrating sometimes having to visit tens of different websites trying to find places that actually seem worth visiting. I hope you are able to fill your itinerary with what you find here and have a wonderful trip to Peru!
